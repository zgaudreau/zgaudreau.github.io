A minimal front-matter-only template at `docs/blog-post-template.md` for new Jekyll blog posts.

Fields: `layout` (post), `page_class` (page-post), `title`, `date` (YYYY-MM-DD placeholder), `category`, `excerpt`. No scaffolded body sections — free-form content only.
