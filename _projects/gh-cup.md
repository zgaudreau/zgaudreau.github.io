A live scoring app for the Green Harbor Cup — our annual golf tournament at Green Harbor Golf Club. Four players, 18 holes, one leaderboard.

Built with React, Vite, and Firebase. Scores update in real time as they're entered. The leaderboard tracks cumulative totals and highlights the leader on each hole.
